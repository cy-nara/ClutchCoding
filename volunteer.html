Our Locations:

Clutch Coding currently offers coding classes at two locations in the Houston area: McGovern-Stella Link Neighborhood Library and Carnegie Neighborhood Library and Center for Learning. At each location, classes are designed to provide a focused learning environment, with a maximum of 8 students per session to ensure individualized attention and hands-on support. These classes introduce elementary school students to the basics of coding, fostering a love for technology and learning.

Looking ahead, Clutch Coding aims to expand its reach by offering classes in additional locations across Texas and nationwide. The goal is to bring coding education to more communities, making it accessible to young learners everywhere, and continuing to build a future where every child has the opportunity to develop crucial tech skills.
